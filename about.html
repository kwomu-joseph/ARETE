About Arete Church TJM

Faith • Hope • Love — since 1962

Our Story

Arete Church TJM was founded in 1962 by a small group of believers who longed for a church where every person — regardless of background — could encounter the love of God. Over six decades later, we have grown into a vibrant multi-generational community committed to worship, discipleship and service.
