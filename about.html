Our Story

Arete Church The Jesus Movement was founded in 2016 by the lead Pastor Doctor Reverend Isaac Nkuusi. Who at the time was a member of peniel Ministries Mbale. Spirit led, he came up with the thought of building a healthy multiplying church whose foundation was family and the church.

Who are we?

Arete is a dynamic, oikos-based, life transforming, generational impacting, movement of the gospel, Discipleship and church planting.

favorite

What do we do?

We exist for the purpose of recruiting people from the kingdom of darkness, equipping them for life and work of ministry, to send them into unreached communities in the world, to transform them by ministering the love of God
